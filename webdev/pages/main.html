Our Mission

Our mission is to collect and provide non perishable food products to soup kitchens around Florida. We intend to help soup kitchens during the covid-19 pandemic as many are running out of raw materials to operate. We are a non-profit organization aimed at helping the community of the homeless and underprivileged.
